# 0.19.2 - Deeper AppGallery paging + "most ranked" rename

Two small tweaks on the AppGallery integration:

## `DEFAULT_MAX_PAGES`: 12 → 20

The biggest categories on AppGallery - Tools, Entertainment, the
editorial Top page - kept saturating the previous 12-page cap. Each
page is 25 items, so the ceiling-per-category went from 300 to 500.

In practice the small categories (Cars, Travel) still stop themselves
with `hasNextPage=0` after one or two pages - the cap only matters
for the heavy ones.

## `top` → `most_ranked`

The URI we walk for the cross-category top list
(`automore|doublecolumncard|903219|A02000`) is the same one
AppGallery's web UI labels **Most Ranked** under its Top tab. Renamed
the catalog category from `top` to `most_ranked` so the dropdown
matches what users see in AppGallery itself.

## Catalog size on the live data

| | v0.19.1 (12 pages) | v0.19.2 (20 pages) |
|--|--:|--:|
| raw items pulled | 911 | 1025 |
| unique after dedup | 783 | 864 |
| `most_ranked` alone (raw) | ~100 | 414 |

Cold fetch is now ~33 s (was ~30 s); the 24 h disk cache makes
subsequent launches instant either way.

Version: 0.19.1 → 0.19.2.
